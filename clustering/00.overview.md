# Clustering & cell-type annotation

How the 86,689 nuclei were clustered into **35 major types** and **206 subtypes**, and how
those labels were annotated. The two modalities — **mCG** (5-kb CG methylation → LSI) and
**3C** (100-kb contacts → per-chromosome PCA) — are integrated across the two donors of each
group with ALLCools `SeuratIntegration` (`find_anchor` → `integrate`), then jointly
consensus-clustered. Clustering proceeds as a **bootstrapping loop**: coarse anchors seed a
preliminary atlas, whose clusters define finer groups that yield **refined** anchors for the
final atlas.

```
(1) tissue-level clustering ──coarse per-donor anchors──► (2) L1pre preliminary all-cell atlas
         tissue/L1/{tissue}/                                        merged/L1pre/
                                                                          │ preliminary L1 + ClusterTissue
                                                                          ▼
(3) tissue-cluster-level refinement ◄── finer groups ──►  refined anchors (geosketch, leiden)
         tissue/L1/{tissue}/02–05, tissue/L2/{tissue}-{subtype}/
                                                                          ▼
(4) all-cell L1 clustering ──aggregate refined anchors──► split into 36 major types
         merged/L1/                                                       │
                                                                          ▼
(5) per-major-type L2 clustering ──► (6) L2any→L2both merge ──► (7) annotation + figures
         merged/L2/{ct}/                merged/L2_hiconly/{ct}/            merged/summary
```

## Anchor identification (what "find anchors" means)
`integrator.find_anchor(adata_list, ...)` computes Seurat-style mutual-nearest-neighbor
anchors between the two donors of a group in a shared reduced space (mCG: LSI-CCA; 3C:
PCA-CCA), scoring each anchor by cross-donor distance (`dist_pc`). **Coarse** anchors (stage 1)
are found per whole tissue; **refined** anchors (stage 3) are found per preliminary
tissue-cluster (`tissue/L2/{tissue}-{subtype}/`) and re-scored with leiden-informed distances,
then thresholded (`dist_pc` < p90) when aggregated into the final L1/L2 embeddings.
