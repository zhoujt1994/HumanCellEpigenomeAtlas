F. Legend-text caveats#

The extracted manuscript legends are truncated for Fig. 1 (after A), Fig. 3 (after E), Fig. 4 (after F), Fig. 6 (after E), and Fig. S32 (after A), plus finer letters of S12/S14. Panel-letter assignments in those ranges are inferred from notebook content and the README, and are marked ⚠ in the chapters. The content is present in code; only the exact letter↔cell binding is unverified.

What to do with this list

Category B (missing plot cells) is the highest-value to fix before release — the analysis already ran, only the final savefig is gone. Category C just needs a savefig(...) added. Category D needs the author to point at the original notebook.
